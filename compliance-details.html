How we respect LinkedIn's platform and terms

🤝 LinkedIn Terms of Service Compliance

We are committed to full compliance with LinkedIn's Terms of Service. This extension operates within LinkedIn's guidelines and respects their platform policies.

🎯

Personal Use Only

  • Individual productivity and organization
  • Personal note-taking and reference
  • No commercial or business intelligence use
  • No lead generation or sales prospecting
  • No bulk data collection or mining
🚫

No Automation

  • No automated likes or reactions
  • No automated comments or messages
  • No automated connection requests
  • No automated profile visits
  • Only passive content saving
⏱️

Respectful Rate Limits

  • Maximum 20 posts per minute
  • Automatic throttling when limits reached
  • Queue system to prevent data loss
  • No aggressive or rapid scraping
  • Server-friendly request patterns
👁️

Public Content Only

  • Only saves content visible in your feed
  • No access to private posts or messages
  • No access to restricted content
  • No profile scraping or data mining
  • Respects LinkedIn's content visibility rules

🛡️ Technical Compliance Measures

🔐 Data Protection

All data stored locally on your device only. No transmission to external servers or third parties.

🚦 Rate Limiting

Smart rate limiting system with queuing to respect LinkedIn's servers and avoid being flagged as automated behavior.

🎯 Targeted Operation

Only operates on LinkedIn feed pages. No monitoring of profiles, messages, or other restricted areas.

✋ User Control

Complete user control with ability to disable, pause, or completely remove all data at any time.
