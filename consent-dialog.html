LinkedIn Post Saver

📋 What This Extension Does

  • ✅ Saves publicly visible LinkedIn posts as you scroll
  • ✅ Stores all data locally on your device only
  • ✅ No data sent to external servers or third parties
  • ✅ You control your data (export, delete, or disable anytime)

⚖️ Terms & Compliance

  • 🎯 Personal Use Only: This extension is for personal productivity
  • 🤝 LinkedIn ToS: We respect LinkedIn's Terms of Service
  • 🚫 No Automation: We don't automate likes, comments, or connections
  • ⏱️ Rate Limited: Maximum 5 posts per 10 seconds to be respectful
  • 🔍 Public Content Only: Only saves what you can already see

🛡️ Your Privacy Rights

Data Collection: Only post content, author info, and timestamps

Data Storage: Chrome's local storage on your device

Data Sharing: Never shared with anyone

Data Deletion: Delete all data anytime in settings

Consent Withdrawal: Disable extension anytime
